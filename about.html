🛡️ About CrowdSafe

A community-powered emergency alert system for a safer campus.

🔍 How it Works

Users can instantly report emergencies — accidents, fires, medical issues — via a simple form. Reports appear immediately on the live dashboard, enabling rapid community awareness and response.

🎯 Our Mission

To create a network of vigilance where information flows freely to ensure everyone's safety. By leveraging the power of the crowd, we can respond faster than traditional methods.

🔒 Privacy & Trust

All reports are verified by campus administrators before being marked as confirmed. We validate reporter contact details to prevent misuse and maintain community trust.
